Visit Vallejo

Enjoy culture and food in this vibrant district from the north bay of California.

Top three gems in Vallejo

  • Six Flags Discovery Kingdom

    Our theme park is a great place to bring the family for rides, games, animals, and much more!

  • P.A.L. Boxing Gym

    This gym is for all ages where you can train or watch athletes "punch with purpose".

  • Food Stands

    Various Mexican food stands with pastor meat straight from the trompo, served with hand-made flour tortillas!
